**Bottom line:** the per-predictor *associations* reproduce the abstract within
rounding — lower iliopsoas volume and signal are independently associated with
non-home discharge. But two corrections change the thesis: (1) the deep-paraspinal
opposite-direction OR is a **suppression artifact** (univariable OR 0.96, p=.82),
not a real effect; (2) against the clinical model, morphometry adds **no**
discrimination, reclassification, or net benefit (IDI NS; incremental NB ≈ 0). The
paper is framed as an **etiologic association study**, not prediction-improvement.
